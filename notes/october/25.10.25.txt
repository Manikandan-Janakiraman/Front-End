Box Model:
-> Clean and clear Layout Method.
-> Most usable tags div in html 
-> margin, padding, border,content (Rule)
-> class selector means . will use 

Padding : content around the spacing (Invisible) 
Border: Intermediate between padding and margin, thicknes or line around he element. (Visible)
Margin: Border around the space, Space outside the border  (Invisible) 

Box Model Rule:
1. margin
2. border
3. padding
4. content
