Chennai Jobs List for outsiders:

Delivery & Logistics (Gig/Field Jobs)

Companies: Swiggy, Zomato, Dunzo, Zepto, Blinkit, Amazon, Flipkart
Type: Delivery partners, warehouse pickers, packers, loaders
Growth: +30–40% in last 3 years (massive rise in gig jobs)
Why open to outsiders: Training not required; anyone with a smartphone & 2-wheeler can start.
Average monthly income: ₹12,000 – ₹25,000

Construction & Real Estate

Companies: L&T, Shapoorji Pallonji, DLF, small contractors
Jobs: Mason, electrician, plumber, painter, helper, crane operator
Growth: +15% in 2024 (Chennai metro, housing projects)
Why open to outsiders: High labour demand; many workers come from Bihar, Odisha, Jharkhand, AP.
Income: ₹15,000 – ₹30,000 + overtime & housing

Manufacturing & Factory Jobs

Locations: Sriperumbudur, Oragadam, Chengalpattu, Hosur (near Chennai)
Companies: Foxconn, Hyundai, TVS, Dell, Nokia, Saint-Gobain
Jobs: Machine operator, quality checker, assembly line helper
Growth: +20% (electronics & EV sector expanding)
Why open to outsiders: Big hiring through contractors; simple skill-based roles
Salary: ₹14,000 – ₹25,000

Hotel, Catering & Food Services

Companies: Saravana Bhavan, A2B, Taj, ITC, OYO, local restaurants
Jobs: Cook helper, waiter, room service, housekeeping
Growth: +10–12% (tourism & hospitality rebound)
Why open to outsiders: Lodging & food often included
Salary: ₹10,000 – ₹20,000 + tips

Security & Maintenance Jobs

Companies: G4S, Topsgrup, private apartments, malls, hospitals
Jobs: Security guard, cleaning staff, maintenance helper
Growth: +8–10% per year
Why open to outsiders: Continuous demand, minimal entry barrier
Income: ₹10,000 – ₹18,000

BPO / Call Centre / Support Jobs

Companies: Tech Mahindra, TCS, Wipro, HGS, Sutherland, Infosys BPM
Jobs: Customer care, tele-caller, chat support, data entry
Growth: +12–15%
Why open to outsiders: English or regional-language speakers welcome; training provided
Salary: ₹15,000 – ₹30,000

Retail & Mall Jobs

Companies: Reliance Trends, DMart, Big Bazaar, Decathlon, malls
Jobs: Cashier, billing staff, sales associate, stock manager
Growth: +10–12%
Why open to outsiders: Basic education enough; high churn so frequent openings
Salary: ₹12,000 – ₹22,000


Transport & Cab Services

Platforms: Ola, Uber, Rapido, Porter
Jobs: Driver partners (cab, bike, goods transport)
Growth: +25% (especially in tier-1 cities)
Why open to outsiders: Driver license + own vehicle or rental
Income: ₹20,000 – ₹45,000 depending on hours


Seasonal & Contract Jobs (Rural to Urban)

Examples:
Festival sales staff (Diwali, Pongal, New Year)

Event setup & cleaning
Wedding hall assistants, decorators
Duration: 1–3 months per contract
Pay: ₹500 – ₹1,000/day
